Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my work?

You can save your work by clicking the save button in the top toolbar or using the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+S (Cmd+S on Mac).

What file formats are supported?

We support common 3D file formats including OBJ, FBX, STL, and GLTF. You can also export to various image formats for renders.

How do I share my projects?

You can share your projects by exporting them or using the built-in sharing feature to generate a shareable link.
